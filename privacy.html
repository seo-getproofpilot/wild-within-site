The legal stuff. Plain language where possible.

About this website

This website is provided by The Wild Within Therapy & Coaching, owned and operated by Alicia Wright, LCSW (Licensed Clinical Social Worker, Arizona). It is for informational purposes only. Visiting this site, sending a message through the contact form, or reading the content does not create a therapist-client relationship.

Professional disclaimer

The content on this website is not medical advice, mental health diagnosis, or treatment. It is not a substitute for individualized care from a licensed mental health or medical provider. Always consult a qualified professional about your specific situation. Outcomes from therapy, coaching, and other services depend on many factors and vary from person to person. No specific result is guaranteed.

Therapy is licensed in Arizona only

Alicia Wright is licensed to provide psychotherapy services in the state of Arizona. Therapy services (including ketamine-assisted psychotherapy) are only available to clients who are physically located in Arizona at the time of the session. Coaching, tantra coaching, workshops, and chakra healing are not state-licensed services and may be available outside Arizona.

Coaching is not therapy

Coaching services offered through The Wild Within are not therapy and do not treat mental health conditions. Coaching is forward-looking, goal-oriented work focused on growth, accountability, and personal development. If you are experiencing a mental health concern, please seek therapy with a licensed clinician.

Ketamine-Assisted Psychotherapy (KAP) disclaimers

Ketamine-Assisted Psychotherapy at The Wild Within is provided under medical supervision in collaboration with a prescribing physician. Ketamine is FDA-approved and legal when administered under medical supervision. Medical screening is required, and contraindications exist (including but not limited to certain cardiovascular conditions, untreated thyroid issues, and active psychosis). KAP is not appropriate for everyone. Results vary, and not all clients will experience benefit. Full informed consent is part of the intake process.

Privacy & data handling

Contact form: Information you submit through the website contact form is sent to Alicia via email. Please do not include sensitive health information in this message. The form is not HIPAA-secured. Detailed health information is collected through a secure, HIPAA-compliant client portal once we begin working together.

Cookies & analytics: This site may use basic analytics to understand how visitors use the site. We do not sell or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

HIPAA-protected information: All clinical records and protected health information (PHI) are stored in our HIPAA-compliant client portal and clinical record system, governed by HIPAA, 42 CFR Part 2 (where applicable), and Arizona state law.

Confidentiality & limits

What you share in therapy or coaching is confidential, with the following legally required exceptions: (1) imminent danger of harm to self or others; (2) suspected abuse or neglect of a minor, elderly, or vulnerable adult; (3) court order. Confidentiality is reviewed in full at the beginning of services.

Crisis resources

The Wild Within is not a crisis service. If you are in immediate danger or having thoughts of harming yourself, please reach out for immediate support:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency: Call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room
