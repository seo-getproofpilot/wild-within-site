Workshops & Healing Circles · Mesa, AZ

Healing isn't meant to happen alone.

Community has its own medicine. Workshops, healing circles, and group experiences with Alicia Wright, LCSW.

✦

Some of the most powerful work happens in a room full of people who are all doing it together.

There's something about a circle of humans choosing the same brave thing at the same time. Something a one-on-one session, no matter how good, can't quite reach. The unspoken permission. The mirror of seeing your own story in someone else's face. The way the body settles when it realizes it's not the only one carrying this.

That's what workshops at The Wild Within are for. Real, embodied, community-rooted healing in Mesa, AZ, and beyond.

What we offer

I host a rotating mix of group experiences throughout the year. Some are one-time intensives. Some are ongoing circles. Some are virtual, some are in-person at the Mesa office, and some happen out in the desert under the sky.

What makes these different

There are a lot of wellness workshops out there. Sound baths, yoga retreats, cacao ceremonies. Some of them are beautiful, and some of them stay on the surface. What I offer is different because I'm not just a facilitator. I'm a licensed clinical social worker with years of trauma-informed training, somatic work, and experience holding space for people in the hardest moments of their lives. That changes the depth of what's possible in a group.

When something comes up for you in a breathwork session or a healing circle, I know how to hold it. I know how to keep the room safe for everyone without shutting down what's real. I know the difference between a release that needs to move and a trauma response that needs containment. Most workshop leaders don't have that training. It's not a knock on them. It's that the container I create has a clinical floor underneath it, and that means you can go deeper without worrying about falling through.

Groups are small on purpose. I'm not interested in packing a room with 50 people and calling it community. Real connection happens when you can see every face, when the facilitator knows your name, when there's enough space for your experience to actually matter. That's what I hold space for. If you've been to a big retreat and felt like you were just part of the crowd, this will feel different.

The power of showing up together

There's something that shifts when you realize you're not the only one carrying this. A healing circle holds that mirror, and the permission that comes with being seen by people who understand. Small groups, real presence, held with care.

What to expect

Group sizes are intentionally small. Usually 6 to 12 people. Confidentiality is the floor; everything that happens in the room stays in the room. You don't need any prior experience with therapy, breathwork, or movement to attend. You just need to bring your willingness to show up.

I'll always tell you ahead of time exactly what we'll be doing, what to wear, what to bring, and what kind of emotional and physical experience to expect. There's never anything sprung on you in the middle of a workshop.

Common Questions

FAQ

What happens in a healing circle?

Healing circles are small, themed group experiences. We usually open with grounding, move into the work of the day (which varies. Could be sharing, breathwork, movement, or guided practice), and close with integration. Confidentiality is held strictly.

Do I need to be in therapy to attend?

No. Workshops are open to anyone willing to show up and engage. Some attendees are also in therapy (with me or someone else); some are not. Both are welcome.

How big are the groups?

Most groups are intentionally small . 6 to 12 people. Some are larger for specific events. Each event listing tells you the group size.

How much do workshops cost?

It varies by workshop. Most fall in the $40–$200 range depending on length and format. Pricing is listed on each event.
