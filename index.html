Meet Alicia

I'm not here to fix you. You were never broken.

I'm here because I've walked my own healing path — through global travel, the Air Force, working with at-risk youth, and navigating my own trauma. That journey taught me that healing isn't clinical. It's wild, human, and deeply personal.

At The Wild Within, I help you rediscover who you have always been and co-create life from an authentic vision. Whether we're working through trauma cycles, reconnecting in your relationship, or exploring the sacred through tantric practices — I meet you where you are with honesty, warmth, and zero judgment.

My Approach

This isn't therapy from a textbook. It's therapy from the heart.

I flow authentic with my people. That means I don't hide behind clinical language or scripted responses. I show up as a human sitting with another human — because that's where real healing lives.

My work is trauma-informed and holistically grounded, drawing from somatic practices, energy healing, tantric principles, and evidence-based approaches. Whether it's a 50-minute session or an extended intensive, every moment is shaped around what you actually need.
